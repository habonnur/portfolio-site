First person, short sentences, no "passionate/leverage/seamless." Helvetica
lines carry the confidence (short, declarative, final period); Baskerville
carries the warmth and the explanation; result lines stay literal — numbers
and facts only, separated by `·`. Every number traceable. AI shows up as
evidence, never as aesthetic.

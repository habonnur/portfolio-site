07 · Guardrails for the design itself

Do

One signature motion moment (the neon flicker in the hero). Scroll reveals on section entry only. Glow as hover feedback.

Do

Cream panels stay matte and print-like: thin borders, small radii (4–8px), generous padding, no glow.

Don't

No purple gradients, no glassmorphism, no more than ~3 cyan elements per screen, no motion on body text.

Don't

If a section has scroll reveal + hover + flicker — remove one. Restraint reads senior.
